SRINAGAR

Amously known as 'Heaven on Earth, Srinagar is located in the union territory of Jammu & Kashmir. Srinagar is known for the stationary houseboats and gondola-type rowboats- Shikaras on Dal Lake.

MANALI

Explore the enchanting beauty of Manali and its surrounding areasincluding the iconic Solang Valley, the spiritual town of Manikaran, and the picturesque town of Kullu.

Leh Ladakh

Extending from the Siachen Glacier to the main Great Himalayas, Ladakh is a land like no other. Dominated by dramatic landscapes, Ladakh is known as the world's coldest desert.
